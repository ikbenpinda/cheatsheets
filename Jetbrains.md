#Cheatsheets // Jetbrains IDEs

Hotkeys for default keymap.

###### Surround with:

    CTRL+SHIFT+T
    
###### Suggestions:

    CTRL+SPACE

###### Show documentation:

    CTRL+Q

###### Show parameters:

    CTRL+P

###### Project settings ("Project Structure"):

    CTRL+SHIFT+ALT+S

###### Show files:

    ALT+1
    
###### "Run"-window:

    ALT+4
    
###### Rename:

    ALT+R, R
